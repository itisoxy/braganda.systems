The Problem

Leads Go Cold
in Minutes.

Research consistently shows that the chance of qualifying a lead drops dramatically within the first 5 minutes of enquiry. Most businesses respond in hours. The ones that win respond in seconds.

The problem isn't your team — it's the process. Manual response workflows, CRM delays, and human handoffs mean even the best sales teams are fighting with one hand tied behind their back.
