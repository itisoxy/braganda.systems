The Problem

Prospects Go
Quiet. Revenue
Disappears.

The average deal requires 5–8 touchpoints to close. Most sales teams manage two or three before moving on. The rest falls into a gap — not because nobody cared, but because there was no system to catch it.

How It Works
Step 01

Trigger

A prospect takes an action — enquiry, download, call, no-show — and a sequence begins automatically.

Step 02

Personalise

AI tailors each message based on what we know about the contact — their source, behaviour, and stage.

Step 03

Convert

The sequence runs until the prospect converts, books, or opts out — with human handoff at the right moment.

Benefits

The Impact.

  • No lead falls through the cracks — every contact gets the right touchpoints.

  • Higher conversion rates from consistent, timely follow-up.

  • Personalised at scale — sequences feel human, even when automated.

  • Freed sales team — focus on closing, not chasing.

  • Full reporting on open rates, replies, and conversions at every stage.
